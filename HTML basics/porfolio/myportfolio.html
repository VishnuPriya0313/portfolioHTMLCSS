Who I am

I'm a Software Engineer who crossed over from Electronics & Communication Engineering into backend development — and never looked back. I specialise in Java, Spring Boot, microservices architecture, and AI-integrated backend systems, with a strong grounding in distributed systems and system design.

Currently at Tabner Global, I work on KonfigAI — a no-code configuration management platform — where I've built AI-powered tools using LLM integration, MCP, agent orchestration, and sub-agent based architecture alongside backend platform work.

My recent work includes integrating OpenAI and Ollama APIs, improving LLM responses with product-specific context, building application lifecycle tools, and supporting configuration workflows through modular agent execution.

Before that, I spent two years at Infosys building payment, cart, and address management services for a large U.S. e-commerce platform. I hold a Master's in Computer Science from Lewis University, IL (GPA 3.7) and a Bachelor's in ECE from JNTU, India.

I care about clean code, solid test coverage, and systems that scale. Outside work I enjoy contributing to the Lewis Indian Association and mentoring at college tech fests.
